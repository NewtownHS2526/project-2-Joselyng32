Hip-Hop music

Hip hop originated in the Bronx, New York City, in the early 1970s among Black and Latino youth, emerging from social and economic challenges as a form of creative expression. It began at block parties with DJs like DJ Kool Herc creating the "merry-go-round" technique to extend instrumental breaks for dancers, laying the foundation for the culture's four main elements: DJing, MCing (rapping), graffiti, and breakdancing.
